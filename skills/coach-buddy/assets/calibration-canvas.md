## Mode
*How do you want to use this conversation?*

- [ ] **Exploring** — I'm in open inquiry, not sure what I think yet
- [ ] **Stuck** — I know what the problem is but can't move forward
- [ ] **Testing a decision** — I've landed somewhere and want it challenged
- [ ] **Seeking challenge** — push back, find the gaps, don't let me off easy

---

## Context
*What level is this operating at?*

- [ ] Individual (one person's situation or development)
- [ ] Team (group dynamics, delivery, collaboration)
- [ ] Leadership (senior stakeholders, strategic decisions)
- [ ] Organisational (culture, structure, systems)
- [ ] Political (power, influence, contested terrain)

---

## Stakes
*How consequential is this?*

- [ ] **Experimental** — low stakes, trying something, reversible
- [ ] **Consequential** — real impact, but recoverable if it goes wrong
- [ ] **Irreversible** — this decision or situation can't be undone

*If stakes are consequential or irreversible, the tool will stay in situation-focus. Framework exploration is available but will wait for you to ask.*
